# CEFR B2 exam — format facts

Factual description of the format of the Cambridge English B2-level
qualification, marketed as "B2 First" (name used nominatively). No official
test content. Figures are for orientation; confirm details with the exam
provider.

**Scale:** Cambridge English Scale. B2 ≈ 160–179; 180–190 shows C1 ability
(Grade A); 140–159 reports at B1. Papers: Reading & Use of English 40%,
Writing / Listening / Speaking 20% each.

**Delivery:** paper and digital versions share the same format; Speaking is
always face-to-face with an examiner.

## Reading & Use of English — 75 min · 7 parts · 52 questions

| Part | Items | Task type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 8 | Multiple-choice cloze (vocabulary, collocation) |
| 2 | 8 | Open cloze (one word per gap, grammar) |
| 3 | 8 | Word formation (change the form of a given root) |
| 4 | 6 | Key word transformations (rewrite using a given word, 2–5 words) |
| 5 | 6 | Long text: 4-option multiple choice |
| 6 | 6 | Gapped text: reinsert removed sentences |
| 7 | 10 | Multiple matching: locate information across texts/sections |

## Writing — 80 min · 2 parts · 140–190 words each

| Part | Task |
|---|---|
| 1 | Essay with two given content points plus one of your own (compulsory) |
| 2 | Choice: article, email/letter, report or review |

Assessed on content, communicative achievement, organisation and language
(criterion names are public).

## Listening — ~40 min · 4 parts · 30 questions

| Part | Items | Task type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 8 | Short unrelated extracts: 3-option multiple choice |
| 2 | 10 | Monologue: sentence completion |
| 3 | 5 | Five themed monologues: multiple matching |
| 4 | 7 | Interview/discussion: 3-option multiple choice |

## Speaking — ~14 min · 4 parts · in pairs

| Part | Time | Format |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 2 min | Interview: personal questions |
| 2 | 4 min | Long turn: compare two photographs (1 min) + 30 s partner question |
| 3 | 4 min | Collaborative task: discuss written prompts, reach a decision |
| 4 | 4 min | Discussion of questions related to Part 3 |
